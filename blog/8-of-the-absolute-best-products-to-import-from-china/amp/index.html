8 Of The Absolute Best Products To Import From China

BorderBuddy
4 years ago

There is a boom in small home businesses that rely on Chinese imports. There are three reasons:

  • The cost is very attractive, which makes for a large profit margin.
  • There are many reliable suppliers. 
  • You can run a business from your home or your garage with minimal overhead, no matter where you live. 

For those who are new to importing, there are several important factors to take into consideration before you start importing from China. Finding trustworthy suppliers with good prices is the first step. We like Alibaba.com.

Why?

Alibaba is a very popular Asian marketplace connecting international online sellers with suppliers and manufacturers. Many eBay, Shopify, and Amazon sellers source their goods on Alibaba. Gold members have been legally registered and vetted, so if you’re looking to eliminate hassles, we recommend searching only gold members. 

Now that you’re ready to consider your importing options, remember the number one rule in any kind of entrepreneurial endeavor.

And that is: 

Know your market. Who are you selling to? Will there be a buyer for your product? Do some comparative shopping online. Know your customer, and know your competition. 

If this is your first rodeo, we suggest you start small, like under 250 CAD. This will keep your risk low, and also allow for less-expensive shipping. Remember, higher-ticket items are usually more difficult and more costly to ship. 

Don’t opt for seasonal items that you might get stuck with for the rest of the year.

To learn more about local or global trends among online shoppers, check out these tools which can be helpful on this quest: Google Trends, Alexa, and Ahrefs.

The bottom line? 

The more research you do, the more in tune you will be with what products are wanted and needed out there. Read the news and pay attention to trends. Because the internet opens up so many possibilities, it’s an exciting time to expand beyond borders in a business where you can set your own hours, and reach millions of customers overnight.
